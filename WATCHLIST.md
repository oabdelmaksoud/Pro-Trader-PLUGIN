## Tier 3 — Dynamic Universe (scanned fresh each session)
No fixed list — Cooper generates this each morning from:

1. **S&P 500 pre-market movers** — top 10 gainers/losers with volume
2. **Earnings reporters** — any company reporting that day or previous evening
3. **Unusual options activity** — tickers with >3x average options volume
4. **Analyst upgrades/downgrades** — any significant rating change pre-market
5. **News catalysts** — M&A, FDA approvals, product launches, macro events
6. **Sector momentum** — leading stocks in the strongest sector of the day
7. **Short squeeze candidates** — high short interest + unusual call buying
8. **Gap plays** — stocks gapping >3% at open with catalyst

## Scoring Priority
Tier 3 > Tier 1 > Tier 2
(Fresh catalysts beat standing watchlist — a random stock with an earnings beat beats AAPL doing nothing)
